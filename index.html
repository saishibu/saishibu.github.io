  • Bio:

    I am a Research Associate with over seven years of experience at the Center for Wireless Networks and Applications (AmritaWNA), Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham. I am passionate about using blockchain technology to create smart energy solutions that benefit society and the environment. My research interests include blockchain, cryptocurrency, NFTs, Metaverse, smart energy distribution, lightweight middleware development, machine learning applications in the energy domain, and incentive-based energy trading in micro and nanogrids. I also enjoy teaching courses on blockchain technology, the internet of things, python programming, and research methodology at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham. I love sharing my knowledge and experience with students who are eager to learn new skills and technologies. I have a master’s degree in Power Electronics from Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Coimbatore Campus and a bachelor’s degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Amritapuri Campus. If you are interested in collaborating with me on any of these topics or want to learn more about my work, please feel free to contact me via LinkedIn or email.
